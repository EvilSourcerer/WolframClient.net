First off, I'd like to let you know that we get way more suggestions than we can actually implement. So it's probably going to take quite a while until we get to your suggestion, provided that we accept it at all.

Second, we only use our GitHub issue tackers to handle suggestions - and we don't accept suggestions submitted through anything else. Do NOT email me about suggestions! If you do, I'll ignore the suggestion and mark your email as spam.

Third, each one of our issue trackers has its own set of rules that you have to follow. If you don't, we will ban your account from all of our issue trackers. Make sure that you read and understand those rules before posting anything.

Still interested? Here's a list of our Wolfram-related GitHub repositories. Find the one that's right for your suggestion, read through the rules and then open an issue there. You will also need a GitHub account if you don't have one already.
