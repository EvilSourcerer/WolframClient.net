About Wolfram

Wolfram is a hacked client for Minecraft that allows you to do all kinds of cheats and hacks that the game normally wouldn't allow, such as flying in Surivival Mode, finding ores with X-Ray vision and automatically attacking enemies.

The Wolfram Client is mostly known for its large collection of hacks, its high-quality NoCheat+ bypasses, a beautiful user interface and its built-in OptiFine integration.

Wolfram was originally created by ShadowSpl0it, who later abandoned the project, and is now maintained by the Wurst-Imperium. We have updated the client to Minecraft 1.9.4 as well as Minecraft 1.10 and we will soon be releasing those versions. Don't miss out!
